Travlendar features

Travel times

Travlendar computes the travel time between events to help you visualize and plan where you'll be and how long it'll take to get there.

Floating events

Not that picky? You can add events like, for example, a thirty minute lunch that occurs daily between 11:30 and 1:00, depending on your day-to-day meetings, we'll find a time for you.

Lightweight extension

We use your Google Calendar and work from there. No need to micromanage and clone events between calendars.

Easily customizable

You can decide how we work for you. We'll be as smart as we can, but you can always set us straight and tell us what you want to happen.
